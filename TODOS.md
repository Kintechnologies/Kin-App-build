## TODO-003 · SMS character limit cap in plaintext adapter

**File:** `apps/web/src/app/api/cron/morning-briefing-sms/route.ts` (to be created)
**Priority:** MEDIUM — before external families onboard

**What:** Cap SMS output at 300 characters. Truncate at the last period before the limit (don't cut mid-sentence).

**Why:** The briefing prompt allows ≤4 sentences with no character limit. A long 4-sentence briefing exceeds 160 chars and splits into a multi-part MMS — costs more per message and some older carrier setups have issues. 300 chars = comfortable 2-message budget with buffer.

**How:** `const trimmed = text.length > 300 ? text.substring(0, text.lastIndexOf('.', 300) + 1) : text`

**Depends on:** Day 1 cron route implementation
