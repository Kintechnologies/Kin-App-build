DATABASE SCHEMA REQUIRED
================================================================================

These tables must exist (built in Tracks A/B):

calendar_connections:
  - id, parent_id, provider, access_token, refresh_token
  - calendar_id, is_read_only, sync_status, last_synced_at

calendar_events:
  - id, parent_id, household_id, title, start_at, end_at
  - is_all_day, location, is_shared, is_work_event
  - external_id, external_source, sync_status

parents:
  - id, household_id, user_id, name, email, timezone, briefing_time

households:
  - id, family_name, created_at

children_activities:
  - id, child_id, household_id, activity_name, days_of_week
  - start_time, end_time, location, season_end_date
