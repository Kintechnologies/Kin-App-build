## Issues Found This Run

### P2-10 (NEW) — `index.tsx` catch fallback text is not spec-approved
**Priority:** P2
**File:** `apps/mobile/app/(tabs)/index.tsx`
**Line:** ~472

**Finding:** The mobile client's catch-only fallback for API failure is:
> "Got your week. I'll flag anything that needs your attention and stay quiet when things look clear."

The spec-approved generic fallback (from `first-use-prompt.md` and implemented in `route.ts`) is:
> "I'm watching your household schedule. The moment something needs your attention, I'll surface it."

The comment says "approved static fallback (§21 compliance maintained)" — but the text is not the IE-approved fallback. This fires only if the entire `/api/first-use` route is unreachable (network failure), so production impact is low. However, the claim of §21 compliance is incorrect for this specific text, and a future editor may rely on that comment.

**Recommended fix:** Replace the catch fallback with the exact spec-approved text: `"I'm watching your household schedule. The moment something needs your attention, I'll surface it."` This matches what `route.ts` returns for `is_fallback: true` and what `first-use-prompt.md` defines as the only acceptable generic fallback.

**Owner:** Lead Eng
