## Section 2: Family tip of the week

If you're in a dual-income household with kids, you know the Sunday scramble — that 6 PM moment when nobody knows what's for dinner, who's driving to practice, or whether there's enough in the account to cover the field trip.

One thing that actually helps: a 15-minute Sunday family sync. Not a meeting — just a walk through the week's calendar together, two dinners decided in advance, and one recurring expense checked. That's it.

It sounds too small to matter. But that 15 minutes prevents about 4 arguments and 2 unnecessary DoorDash orders every week. Over a year, that's real money and a quieter household.

Doesn't require any app. Just 15 minutes and the same night every week.
